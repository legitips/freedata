WELCOME TO FG ₦65,000 WOMEN SMALL SCALE BUSINESS SUPPORT FUNDING REGISTRATION PORTAL.

APPLICATION FORM

DON'T MISS THIS OFFER!!!

Due to high level of complains from Nigerian women, President Bola Ahmed Tinubu have urge that the Ministry Of Finance Zainab Shamsuna Ahmed should open the WOMEN SMALL SCALE BUSINESS SUPPORT scheme. Every Nigerian women is entitled to receive the sum of ₦65,000 for 6 months to startup or equip her small scale business.


Registration Starts 1st July 2024 and ends 27th September 2024
